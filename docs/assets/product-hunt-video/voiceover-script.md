# Product Hunt Video Voiceover Script

Target length: 75-85 seconds.

## 0:00-0:09 Hook

Hi Product Hunt. This is Industry Research Framework, an open-source protocol for AI agents doing longform, source-backed industry research.

## 0:09-0:21 Problem

I built it because long AI research tasks fail in repeatable ways: agents start before scope is clear, mix facts with judgment, leak process notes, and call thin drafts complete.

## 0:21-0:33 Protocol

The framework gives agents a concrete research path: brief gates, state files, source registries, claim tracking, uncertainty notes, staged drafting, review loops, and hard stops.

## 0:33-0:45 Before / After

Without a protocol, you get source lists and plausible summaries. With the framework, every major claim has evidence or a limitation, and the final report is written for readers.

## 0:45-0:57 Evals

It also ships an offline eval layer: task cases, source packs, rubrics, and regression fixtures for process leakage, evidence drift, false completion, and depth collapse.

## 0:57-1:10 Use Flow

You do not need a hosted app. Give the repo to Codex, Claude, Gemini, Cursor, ChatGPT, OpenClaw, or Hermes, and tell the agent to treat `SKILL.md` as the research protocol.

## 1:10-1:23 Ask

I would love feedback from analysts, agent builders, and research writers. Where should this protocol be stricter, lighter, or easier to adopt?
